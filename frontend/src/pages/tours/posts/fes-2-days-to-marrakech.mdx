## Overview

This 2-day express tour from Fes to Marrakech is designed for travelers who want to experience Morocco's Sahara Desert while efficiently traveling between two of the country's most famous imperial cities. Instead of taking a flight or train, you'll journey through spectacular landscapes including the Middle Atlas cedar forests, the dramatic Ziz Valley, the golden dunes of Erg Chebbi, and the UNESCO World Heritage kasbah of Ait Ben Haddou.

Perfect for those with limited time, this fast-paced adventure combines necessary transportation with unforgettable experiences. You'll ride camels at sunset, sleep in a traditional desert camp under the stars, cross the High Atlas Mountains, and witness Morocco's diverse landscapes—all while traveling from the medieval medina of Fes to the vibrant red city of Marrakech.

Arrive at your desert camp:
