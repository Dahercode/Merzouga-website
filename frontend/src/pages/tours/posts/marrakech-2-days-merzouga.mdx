## Overview

Short on time but don't want to miss the magic of the Sahara? This express 2-day tour from Marrakech to Merzouga is designed for travelers who want to experience Morocco's desert with limited time. Despite the quick pace, you'll still enjoy the essential highlights: crossing the High Atlas Mountains, visiting the famous Kasbah Ait Ben Haddou, riding camels across golden dunes, and spending an unforgettable night in a traditional desert camp.

This tour maximizes your time with early departures and efficient routing, ensuring you experience the authentic Sahara without sacrificing the quality of the adventure. Perfect for weekend getaways or travelers continuing to other Moroccan cities.
