## Overview

This 3-day desert tour from Fes to Marrakech is the perfect solution for travelers who want to experience Morocco's Sahara Desert while efficiently traveling between the country's two most iconic imperial cities. Instead of backtracking or taking a quick flight, you'll journey through some of Morocco's most spectacular landscapes—the Middle Atlas Mountains, the vast Sahara Desert, dramatic gorges, and ancient kasbahs.

This one-way tour maximizes your time in Morocco by combining transportation with adventure. You'll experience authentic camel trekking, sleep in a traditional desert camp under the stars, cross the High Atlas Mountains, and visit UNESCO World Heritage sites, all while making your way from the medieval medina of Fes to the vibrant souks of Marrakech.
