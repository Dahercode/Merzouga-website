## Overview

This 2-day express tour from Marrakech to Fes is designed for travelers who want to experience Morocco's Sahara Desert while efficiently traveling between two of the country's most iconic cities. Instead of backtracking or taking a flight, you'll journey through spectacular landscapes including the High Atlas Mountains, UNESCO World Heritage kasbahs, and the golden dunes of Erg Chebbi.

Perfect for those with limited time, this fast-paced adventure combines transportation with unforgettable experiences. You'll cross mountain passes, ride camels at sunset, sleep in a traditional desert camp, and traverse the dramatic Ziz Valley—all while making your way from the red city of Marrakech to the medieval medina of Fes.
