## Overview

Limited time but want to experience the Sahara? This express 2-day tour from Fes to Merzouga is designed for travelers who want an authentic desert adventure without a lengthy time commitment. You'll journey from the ancient medina of Fes through Morocco's diverse landscapes—alpine forests, mountain passes, and palm oases—before arriving at the magnificent Erg Chebbi sand dunes.

Despite the condensed timeframe, this tour doesn't compromise on the essential Sahara experiences: camel trekking at sunset, sleeping in a traditional desert camp under countless stars, and witnessing dawn break over the endless dunes. It's the perfect weekend escape or addition to a longer Moroccan itinerary.
