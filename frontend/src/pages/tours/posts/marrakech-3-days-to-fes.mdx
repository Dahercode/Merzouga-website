## Overview

This 3-day desert tour from Marrakech to Fes is the perfect solution for travelers who want to experience Morocco''s Sahara Desert while efficiently traveling between two of the country''s most iconic imperial cities. Instead of backtracking or taking a quick flight, you''ll journey through spectacular landscapes—the High Atlas Mountains, ancient kasbahs, dramatic gorges, and the endless Sahara.

This one-way tour combines transportation with adventure, allowing you to experience camel trekking, sleep in a desert camp under the stars, visit UNESCO World Heritage sites, and arrive in Fes having truly experienced the magic of Morocco''s diverse landscapes.
