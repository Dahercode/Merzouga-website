## Overview

This 5-day desert tour from Marrakech to Merzouga offers the perfect balance between comprehensive exploration and comfortable pacing. You''ll experience Morocco''s most iconic southern route with extra time to truly immerse yourself in desert culture, including two nights in the Merzouga region—one in a luxury desert camp and one in comfortable accommodation.

The relaxed itinerary allows for genuine cultural encounters, from meeting nomadic families to enjoying Gnawa music, exploring dramatic gorges, and riding camels across the Erg Chebbi dunes. This is the ideal tour for travelers who want depth over speed, memories over miles.
